Welcome to Avantikeya

about us

Avantikeya is a Cultural Creed based in Ujjain, which provides a rostrum to ardently enthusiastic students striving for excellence. Avantikeya has become the name of the game in cultural endeavors where thoughts turn into opportunities which meld with the society. It aims to spread awareness in society through exchange of ideas and by raising debates relevant to the times. Avantikeya stands hand in hand with ethical contemplations and comes down like a ton of bricks against the false aspects of the society. Our vision is to mould our workforce into some of the best leaders that our nation has ever seen by instilling essential qualities like perseverance, dedication and team-work in them.

Motive

Because of the paucity of the chain of command and lack of exposure in our colleges, a rich pool of talent goes unutilized. Avantikeya has emerged as an assembly which offers a route to tame one’s talents apart from academics. Avantikeya, earlier primarily focusing on street plays, has broadened the horizons by including various skills like acting, drama, stage play, dancing, singing, photography, poetry, creative writing, debate, speech, journalism, sketching, painting, web development, etc. Avantikeya is on the crest of the wave which now advances in the field of literature. Avantikeya has been conceived with the idea of raising consciousness about social issues through meaningful theatre and cultural activities.

our initiatives

#SayNoToAddiction

The pioneer performance which was performed during the Annual Function to create an awareness amongst students related to ill effects of drug addiction, one of the fatal knacks amongst the college goers. College authorities took a note on related prevailing conditions and strictly banned vending of nicotine goods.

#Hope

On 17 April, 2015 , Avantikeya negotiated a drama which was an important part of the HIV Awareness Campaign, a Department of Public Health initiative held in Government Engineering College, Ujjain. Key concerns related to the ailment were highlighted and made aware of.

#HindiHainHum

With an aim to invigorate the love for India’s official language among the students, Avantikeya performed a street play focusing on the importance of Hindi on the occasion of Hindi Diwas in the college campus. It was remarkably acknowledged.

#EyeOpener

In the month of October, Avantikeya performed Street play in IIT, Delhi and NIFT, Delhi depicting the importance of sexuality education and awaring the students on the same subject. The effort was highly appreciated by the audience.

#GivingItBack

An interactive session was organized by Avantikeya where seniors mentored and guided the newbies by sharing their life experiences on Engineering. The session was held successfully and also it encouraged the students to get the best out of their college life.

#IlluminateToRejuvenate

Avantikeya with association with Apna Sweets, Ujjain conducted an event on 7th November,2015 to promote the usage of earthen lamps instead of electronic decoration and to celebrate an eco friendly Deepawali. The initiative was immensely applauded by the locals.

#DonateWarmth

Avantikeya in association with Apna Sweets ,Ujjain organized a campaign to help the poverty stricken people to stay warm in the harsh winters. Various woolen donation camps were launched at different corners of the city and local people were urged to donate warmth through interactive sessions and street plays.
